CSR
VISTARA actively contributes towards the social and economic development of the communities. In this regard, various activities are performed

LOCAL EMPLOYMENT

VISTARA is a major employer and encourages local talent to apply for open position. Apart from full time employment, traineeships and internships are offered. Students and fresh graduates can gain invaluable experience.


EDUCATION

VISTARA supports local schools by way of grants scholarships and provision stationary and supplies.


UPGRADING SKILLS

Employees are encouraged to upgrade skills through training programs and continuing education. External as well as internal programs are regularly conducted for all employees. Employees opting for executive education courses are fully supported.


ENVIRONMENT

Every year during rainy season we plant trees at the Agashiv Fort Location and also take care of planted trees regularly
